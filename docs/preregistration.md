# Pre-registration

The canonical, timestamped pre-registration is the OSF record:

**DOI:** [10.17605/OSF.IO/BFQDP](https://doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/BFQDP)
**OSF:** https://osf.io/bfqdp/
**Deposited:** 2026-05-29, before signing the Vivli Data Use Agreement or
entering the secure research environment.

The OSF record is authoritative. This repository implements the plan it
describes; the locked parameters are mirrored in
[`src/amr_sentinel_vivli/config.py`](../src/amr_sentinel_vivli/config.py).

Any change to the pre-registered plan after data access is documented as a
**deviation** (pre-reg §11) in the final report's Methods, with: what changed,
when discovered, why required, and the robustness check confirming it does not
affect headline conclusions.
